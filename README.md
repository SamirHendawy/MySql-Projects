### Projects List

#### 1. [Instagram Clone Project](https://github.com/SamirHendawy/MySql-Projects/tree/main/Instagram%20Clone%20Project)
A complex MySQL project mimicking an Instagram database to perform deep data analysis. It addresses real-world business scenarios and technical challenges.

**Key Analysis Performed:**
* **User Engagement:** Finding the oldest users for loyalty rewards and identifying the most popular registration days for ad campaigns.
* **Inactive User Tracking:** Identifying users who have never posted a photo or commented to target them with re-engagement emails.
* **Contest Winners:** Calculating which photo received the most likes to determine contest winners.
* **Bot Detection:** Writing complex queries to find "Bot" accounts (users who liked every single photo on the platform).
* **Platform Statistics:** Calculating average posts per user and identifying the top 5 most used hashtags for marketing brands.
* **Advanced Metrics:** Using **CTEs** and subqueries to calculate the percentage of users who are inactive vs. active commenters.
